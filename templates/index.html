Welcome to Our Health Portal

Electronic Health Records (EHR) provide a digital version of a patient's paper chart. They make information available instantly and securely to authorized users. EHRs can contain a patient's medical history, diagnoses, medications, treatment plans, immunization dates, allergies, radiology images, and laboratory test results.

Health is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. It is important to maintain good health through regular exercise, balanced nutrition, adequate sleep, and stress management.
